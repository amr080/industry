Trading wallet vs. Launchpad wallet
Deposit
Withdrawal
Transfer

If you wish to use your funds on a specific platform, you may choose to transfer your funds to the corresponding wallet. For instance, if you wish to use the balances in your Trade wallet to participate in the STO launchpad, you need to first transfer your Trade wallet balances to the corresponding asset wallet within the Launchpad wallet.

To transfer funds between your Trade and Launchpad wallets:

  1. Log in to your Denode account, press [Wallet] - [Overview] and choose the wallet you would like to transfer funds from.
  2. Press [Transfer] on the corresponding fiat/cryptocurrency.
  3. Choose the wallet to transfer from and transfer to from the dropdown list of wallets.
  4. Enter the transfer amount in the corresponding fiat currency or cryptocurrency. Please note that you cannot enter a transfer amount that exceeds the available balance shown.
  5. Transfers between Trade wallet and Launchpad wallet takes place instantly and with no fees associated.
  6. Press [Confirm] to complete the transfer.

Once the transfer is successfully processed, you can check your respective wallet balances in the [Overview] tab under the [Wallet] menu.
