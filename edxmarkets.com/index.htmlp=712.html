Sales Operations Intern

  • New York

MEDX is a rapidly growing digital asset technology firm that combines an institution-only marketplace with a central clearinghouse. EDX Markets, our flagship trading venue, is designed to emulate the worlds most sophisticated exchanges, with deep liquidity, firm prices and low trading costs. Non-custodial and non-conflicted, EDX has structured its business to minimize risk for its members while providing a diverse array of operational and capital efficiencies. Backed by some of the worlds leading high-frequency trading and venture capital firms, EDX is actively developing new features and expanding its geographic presence to deliver trusted, liquid and efficient crypto trading experiences for all institutions. To learn more, visit edxmarkets.com.

Role:

EDX Markets is seeking a Sales Operations Intern. This role is hybrid; the in-office component is based in the New York-metro area. The Sales Operations Intern will assist in the operational duties required to support our sales teams functions. The intern will gain knowledge of the crypto financial services industry as well as hands on experience to showcase and develop their professional skills. The internship is paid with a minimum duration of 6 months and targeting 15-20hrs per week.

Responsibilities:
  • Conduct research on prospective clients
  • Support the sales team with data analysis and reporting
  • Maintain sales CRM/pipeline for tracking and updates (Salesforce)
  • Prepare presentations for use during the sales cycle
  • Assist with ongoing marketing initiatives
  • Participate in internal meetings and contribute ideas to team discussions

Qualifications:
  • Junior or senior enrolled in undergraduate program, or in a graduate program
  • Interest in sales, marketing, ops, crypto, financials services, and/or related field
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Excellent data analysis and communication skills (verbal and written)
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment
  • Curious, self-starter attitude
