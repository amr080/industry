The US-Based Spot Venue for Institutions

EDX Markets, with its unique central clearinghouse, efficiently and securely serves institutions, including prime brokers, retail intermediaries and market makers, by aggregating liquidity and employing a single net settlement process that reduces the upfront capital required to trade and minimizes credit risk.

Minimal Risk Through Maximum Control

EDX Markets is purpose-built for security. As a rules-based crypto platform, we maintain careful risk controls while segregating our marketplace from third-party custody functions, creating a safer trading environment for all.
Learn More

Our risk controls include:

  • A clearinghouse risk model that reduces trade settlement risk
  • A non-custodial marketplace
  • A default fund to provide assurance of settlements each day
  • Robust pre- and post-trade risk protections
  • Bankruptcy-remote customer accounts
  • The highest standards of compliance
