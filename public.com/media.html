Zaid Admani

Zaid Admani is a finance content creator who makes witty and insightful videos on TikTok and Instagram. Zaid started making finance content back in 2019 breaking down complex financial concepts into entertaining skits. Zaid's unique blend of humor and financial insight has made him a go-to source for anyone looking to stay informed about the latest business news while enjoying a good laugh.

Hope King

Hope King is Axios’ Senior Business Reporter covering everything from the economy to Elon Musk — including trends in retail, tech, logistics, sustainability and CEO leadership. Hope has conducted interviews with entrepreneur Mark Cuban, U.S. Trade Ambassador Katherine Tai, Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella, Alibaba co-founder Jack Ma, and NBA star Steph Curry, among many more. Hope also appears regularly on CNBC, MSNBC and CBS—and prior to journalism, was a VP of business development on Wall Street.

Ann Berry

Ann is a commentator on global TV networks including the BBC, Bloomberg & CNBC. She also hosts market shows and CEO interviews for Public.com, broadcasting to 3 million US retail subscribers. Ann is founder of advisory firm Threadneedle and General Partner of its technology-focused Opportunity Fund, backed by Alexis Ohanian.

Kyla Scanlon

Kyla is a financial educator and commentator who focuses on analyzing the economy with a human-focused lens. She is the author of “In This Economy” and has written for The New York Times, New York Magazine, and Bloomberg Opinion.

JD Durkin

J.D. Durkin is a host for Public.com and a CBS News Contributor at the New York Stock Exchange. His recent bylines include Sports Illustrated and TheStreet, and he has moderated at conferences like Web Summit in Rio and Lisbon, Collision in Toronto, and Politicon in Nashville. He previously anchored the national primetime show ‘None of the Above’ on Cheddar News, and co-created the original documentary series ‘The Pivot Counties’ while working as White House and Congressional Correspondent.
