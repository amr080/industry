About Beam

Better Payments for Businesses of All Sizes

Who are we?

Since the inception of the modern U.S. financial system, small and medium-sized businesses have been constrained by frameworks designed to favor only the largest players. Traditional banks have built barriers that limit access to affordable global payments and liquidity. Consequently, countless businesses grapple with fragmented, costly, and time-consuming payment processes that stifle their ability to compete.

At Beam, we envision a world where these limitations no longer exist. We see a future where every business – no matter its size – can access the financial tools needed to succeed. By harnessing the power of modern payments technology, we are breaking down the barriers of traditional finance. Beam enables businesses to move money effortlessly, quickly, and affordably. We’re creating a level playing field where all businesses can thrive and reach their fullest potential.

Welcome to the future of opportunity. Welcome to the future of payments: Beam.

Driving $200M+ in Global Transactions
Beam has processed over $200 million in global payments that have enabled small and medium-sized businesses to efficiently manage their financial operations across borders.
