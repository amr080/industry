Frequently Asked Questions

What is Beam Hub?

Beam Hub is your team's command center for efficient workflows and streamlined operations. Our payment solution allows businesses to quickly convert between selected stablecoins, digital assets, and US dollars.

How does it work?

After setting up your account, you’ll receive a list of unique asset addresses for your business. For stablecoins, you’ll find a wallet address for each asset; for fiat, you’ll receive Wire/ACH instructions. To make a transfer, simply send your stablecoins to the designated asset wallet address or initiate a USD Wire/ACH transfer using the provided bank instructions. We’ll then convert the funds into USD or stablecoins and send them directly to your preferred US bank account or wallet address.

How can I get a Beam Hub account?

To get started, please reach out to our Sales team here.

What is the pricing for Beam Hub?

We charge a transaction fee based on the volume of each transfer. Payment rail fees, where applicable, are passed through to you, and you are also responsible for any applicable gas fees.

What regions do you support?

Within the US, we support businesses in all states except New York and Alaska. For businesses located outside the US, we evaluate each case individually. If you’re outside the US and want to discuss options, please contact our Sales team here.

Is Beam Hub for individuals or businesses only?

Beam Hub accounts are designed for businesses only. If you have individual or personal payment needs, please reach out to us at support@getbeam.cash to discuss options.

What conversion flows do you support?

We support both stablecoin-to-USD and USD-to-stablecoin conversions.

Which assets do you support?
  • Fiat: USD (more currencies coming soon!)
  • ‍Stablecoins: USDC.POLYGON, USDC.XLM, USDC.SOL, USDC.ETH, and more. Check out our full list here and feel free to reach out if you’re looking for an asset not listed.
What payment rails do you support?

We support Wires and Next-Day ACH.

How fast are transfers?

Transfers typically take under 24 hours on business days, with some transactions completed within minutes. Payments vary by payment rail: Wires are sent the same day on business days, while ACH transfers are sent the next business day.

What else is included in my Beam Hub dashboard?

Your Beam Hub dashboard provides essential information for managing your transfers and account, including a transaction log, reporting, payment settings, user management, and more.
