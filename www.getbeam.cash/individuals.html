How does it work?

1
Sign up for Beam account
2
Complete identity verification and connect bank account
3
We will provide a Beam address for each of our supported chains
4
Send supported crypto assets to your Beam address within the Beam app or straight from your wallet
5
We will convert to USD and deposit in your bank account or debit card

Who can use Beam for Individuals

You live in the US, in any state.
Except for: New York, Louisiana, Alaska, and Hawaii
You have a US personal checking account or debit card
You have one of our supported assets in a digital wallet
Your identity is verifiable
We will convert to USD and deposit in your bank account or debit card
