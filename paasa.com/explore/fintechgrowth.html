This portfolio invests equally in 10 leading Fintech driving the transformation of global financial services.

  1. Digital Payments & Card Networks:

    Visa, Mastercard and Paypal are positioned to benefit from the global shift from cash to digital transactions and the growth of e-commerce.

  2. Peer-to-Peer Payments & Merchant Services:

    Block Inc. provides innovative payment solutions for small businesses and mobile commerce.

  3. Cryptocurrency & Blockchain Exposure:

    Coinbase Global offers direct exposure to cryptocurrency markets, riding the wave of increasing adoption of digital assets.

  4. Alternative Banking & Lending:

    SoFi Technologies and Affirm Holdings provide services such as digital banking, student loans, and “Buy Now, Pay Later” (BNPL) solutions.

  5. Emerging Market Financial Inclusion:

    Nu Holdings leverages mobile technology to expand financial access in underbanked regions like Latin America.

  6. Retail Investing Disruption:

    Robinhood Markets appeals to the next generation of traders by democratizing investing through commission-free trading.

  7. Payment Infrastructure:

    Fiserv Inc. provides critical financial services infrastructure, enabling seamless transactions for businesses worldwide.


This portfolio is ideal for growth oriented investors with a long time horizon who seek exposure to the ongoing digital transformation of finance. While offering high growth potential, it carries risks of volatility and regulatory uncertainty.
