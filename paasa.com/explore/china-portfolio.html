China portfolio

This portfolio offers diversified exposure to the Chinese economy, specifically targeting key growth sectors such as internet technology, e-commerce, and advanced manufacturing. It aims to capitalize on China's economic transition from export-driven growth to a consumption- and innovation-led model. The combination of broad market and sector-specific ETFs provides a balance between growth potential and diversification, making it suitable for investors seeking exposure to China's long-term economic trajectory.

The allocation to iShares MSCI China UCITS ETF ensures exposure to a diversified set of industries, mitigating sector-specific risks. KraneShares CSI China Internet ETF provides a growth-focused component, driven by the rapid expansion of digital and internet-based services. Invesco MSCI China technology ETF delivers targeted exposure to high-potential technology and innovation sectors.
