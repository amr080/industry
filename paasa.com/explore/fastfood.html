Fast Food

Fast Food

Very high risk

Stocks

This portfolio focuses on leading quick-service restaurants (QSRs) and beverage companies targeting the growing global demand for convenient, affordable, and high-quality dining and drink options. The allocation emphasizes established brands with wide customer bases and high brand loyalty, complemented by emerging growth players in the fast-casual and specialty beverage sectors.

  1. Global Expansion of QSR Chains:

    Companies like McDonald's and Yum Brands (KFC, Taco Bell, Pizza Hut) continue to grow through international expansion and franchising models.

  2. Shift Toward Premium & Specialty Beverages:

    Starbucks and Coca-Cola leverage growing demand for premium coffee, tea, and non-alcoholic beverages globally.

  3. Fast-Casual Dining Trend:

    Chains like Chipotle Mexican Grill and Shake Shack benefit from consumers seeking healthier, customizable options.

  4. Food Delivery Boom:

    Domino's Pizza and Wingstop are well-positioned to capture growth in digital orders and delivery, driven by changing dining habits post-pandemic.

  5. Snack & Beverage Synergy:

    PepsiCo supports its snack and beverage businesses with innovations tailored to changing consumer preferences.
