Corporate bonds

The portfolio combines high-income potential with risk diversification. Its heavy allocation to high-yield bonds enhances returns, while emerging market debt introduces geographic diversity. The smaller allocation to investment-grade bonds provides a safety cushion against market volatility or economic downturns.


  • High-Yield Corporate Bonds (60%)

    iShares $ High Yield Corp Bond UCITS ETF offers exposure to non-investment-grade corporate bonds, providing attractive yields with higher credit risk. These bonds are issued by established corporations seeking to fund growth or refinance debt, appealing to yield-seeking investors.


  • Emerging Market Corporate Bonds (30%)

    iShares J.P. Morgan $ EM Corp Bond UCITS ETF focuses on bonds from corporations in emerging markets. This allocation diversifies credit risk geographically and capitalizes on the higher growth potential and improving creditworthiness of emerging economies.

  • Investment-Grade Corporate Bonds (10%)

    iShares $ Corp Bond UCITS ETF delivers stability through bonds with higher credit ratings, offering a lower-risk component to balance the portfolio’s riskier assets.
