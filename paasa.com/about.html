About us

We believe that access to global markets shouldn't be limited by geography

We believe that access to global markets shouldn't be limited by geography

Paasa makes it easy for anyone in the world to diversify their wealth globally with no headache. Our mission is simple: build a platform that helps you grow your portfolio across the world, using the best technology, data, and financial strategies available.

We’re ex-SoFi, where we helped build one of the most successful all-digital banks in the US. But we saw a gap — global investing was still too complex and out of reach for many. So, we left to solve that problem.

Paasa gives you the tools you need to invest globally: cross-border flows, curated portfolios, and personalized advice. From dollar-denominated accounts to tax-efficient strategies, every part of our platform is designed to help you grow your wealth while hiding the complexity.


We’re fortunate to be backed by respected angels and VCs in the finance world.  If you're excited by a world where financial opportunities are truly global, reach out to us at founders@paasa.com.
