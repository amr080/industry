Developer comments
Rainbow for Firefox is currently in Beta. Your wallets are fully secured and protected, but Firefox currently suffers from performance issues related to modern extension architectures. Our team is hard at work addressing these limitations with Firefox and the broader industry to improve your user experience.

You can report bugs or contact Rainbow support via email at support@rainbow.me or via our online chat: https://rainbow.me/support/extension
