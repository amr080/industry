Digital Asset Insurance

Backed by up to $250MM in insurance

BitGo Trust maintains coverage against loss, theft, and misuse in situations where we hold all keys.
Connect with Us

BitGo maintains coverage

We maintain a $250M insurance policy on digital assets where BitGo Trust Company maintains all of the keys.

Here to help

BitGo pays any and all deductibles since we're the party directly covered by the policy.

If desired, customers can work with BitGo's insurance broker to purchase "excess specie" insurance.

Deep, reputable coverage

BitGo’s insurance policy is provided by a syndicate of insurers in the Lloyd’s of London and European Marketplace.

Lloyd’s of London is a well-known insurer and covers some of the world’s most valuable assets.
