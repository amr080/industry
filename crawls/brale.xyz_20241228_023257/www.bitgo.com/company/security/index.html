Industry-leader for securing digital assets

Protect digital assets from cyber threats and insider threats with our multi-layered security approach.

Security is our foundation and commitment

Founded in 2013 the early days of crypto BitGo pioneered the multi-signature wallet and threshold signature schemes (TSS) offerings. BitGo offers the most secure technology on the global market and safeguards over 800 tokens across multiple blockchains.

BitGo has never experienced a theft of assets under our custody.

The most trusted crypto wallet architecture on the market

  • Self-custody wallets

    Allowing you to take control of your security with wallet and transaction limit policies.

    Self-custody wallets

    BitGos wallet infrastructure seamlessly integrates hot and cold wallets.

  • M-of-N key structure

    Providing the ultimate layer of protection against unauthorized transfers.

    M-of-N key structure

    Our multi-signature setup (M-of-N) ensures that multiple parties must sign to approve transactions.

  • Role-based approvals

    Adding flexibility and control to your asset management.

    Role-based approvals

Bank-grade security controls

Customizable to meet your organizations needs.


  • Policy engine management

    Define customizable rules for fast transaction approval, address whitelisting, and velocity limits ensuring transactions are executed according to your pre-defined parameters.

  • Key management

    Combine the worlds best hardware security modules (HSMs) and strict operational controls to safeguard private keys throughout their lifecycle.

  • Multi-layered security

    Implement multi-layered security controls that validate every transaction and every signer without fail.

Transparent operations
