Globally licensed and regulated

BitGo is designed to secure billions of dollars of digital assets, and takes a no-compromise approach to security with multiple trust companies around the world.

  • United States

    South Dakota

    BitGo Trust Company, Inc. is a qualified custodian regulated by the South Dakota Division of Banking, and also registered as a Money Services Business (MSB) with the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN).

  • United States

    New York

    BitGo New York Trust Company, LLC is a qualified custodian regulated by the New York Department of Financial Services (NYDFS).

  • Asia

    Singapore

    BitGo Singapore Pte. Ltd. is regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) with a Major Payment Institution License.

  • Asia

    Dubai

    BitGo Custody MENA FZE has an initial approval from the Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority for broker-dealer and custody services.

  • Europe

    Germany

    BitGo Europe GmbH is a regulated custodian by the Federal Financial Supervisory Authority (BaFin).

  • Europe

    Switzerland

    BitGo GmbH is a regulated custodian by the Financial Services Standards Association (VQF).

  • Europe

    Spain

    BitGo Europe GmbH is registered with Banco de España.

  • Europe

    Italy

    BitGo Europe GmbH is registered with Organismo Agenti e Mediatori.

  • Europe

    Poland

    BitGo Europe GmbH is registered with Dyrektora Izby Administracji Skarbowej e Katowicah.

  • Europe

    Greece

    BitGo Europe GmbH is registered as a custody Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP) with the Hellenic Securities Market Commission (HCMC).
