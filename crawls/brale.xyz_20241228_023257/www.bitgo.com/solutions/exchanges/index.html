The most efficient solution to:

  • Generate wallets for your users

    Easily integrate our wallets into your UI to create and manage receive addresses via API without extra back-end development.

  • Secure and manage on one platform

    Customized wallets architecture to manage all of your customer funds on one platform with an independent and insured custodian, so that you can focus on scaling.

  • Establish and enforce wallet policies

    Set and enforce wallet policies with user-specific controls, transaction and velocity limits, whitelists, and multi-level approvals to minimize risk and manage access across your exchange.
