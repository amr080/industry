The only federally chartered crypto bank

Anchorage Digital Bank is the only crypto-native bank to hold a charter from the US Office of the Comptroller of the Currency. As an unequivocal qualified custodian, Anchorage Digital Bank works closely with its regulators to meet stringent compliance requirements and to set the industry bar for digital asset operations under federal oversight.

A complete offering

Here are a few reasons why you should choose Anchorage Digital.

Safeguard assets

Security is the foundation of everything we do. Our differentiated custody solution mitigates the risk of human error, and is backed by biometric authentication, behavioral analytics, and robust controls—all without compromising accessibility.

Maximize participation

Safely collect staking rewards, participate in on-chain community governance, and trade 24/7/365 with access to multiple liquidity venues and tight pricing.

Assets supported

Anchorage Digital supports assets that meet our standards of quality and safety. Asset support varies by legal entity, jurisdiction, and service.
