Why restake with BitGo?

  • Select vetted AVS operators

    Choose from a handpicked list of AVS operators vetted by BitGo, and view their strategies, helping you make better informed choices.

  • Manage your restaking activity

    Track your restaked assets in the BitGo UI and generate reports detailing your restaking activity and rewards.

  • Continually collect rewards

    Seamlessly secure and grow your rewards at an annual percentage so your assets don't sit idle.

Keeping your assets safe, one feature at a time

  • Best-in-class Security

    Institutional-grade protections with segregated, bankruptcy-remote accounts through BitGo Trust Company, Inc.

  • Continuous rewards

    Stake EIGEN or restake ETH with curated AVS operators to ensure your assets work harder while staying secure.

  • Curated Operator List

    Access a handpicked list of AVS operators, including familiar players like Figment, Blockdaemon, Kiln, and P2P.

  • Comprehensive Staking Services

    Support for EIGEN custody, staking, and ETH restaking all within the same trusted platform.
