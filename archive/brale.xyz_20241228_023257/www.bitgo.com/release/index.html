Digital assets are here, just not evenly distributed.

The way we interact with finance is fundamentally changing. We now live in a programmable economy.

We're witnessing a once-in-a-generation shift from centralized finance to decentralized finance, where anyone, anywhere, with just a few lines of code, can create new financial products. In 2024, the market cap of crypto surpassed that of the top 10 global banks, signaling the dawn of a new economic era.

Institutions, businesses, and individuals now demand seamless access to digital assets—custody, staking, trading, lending, and borrowing. Next-generation finance necessitates next-generation rails.

Since 2013, BitGo has been engineering the bridge to this new economic standard in a secure, seamless, and compliant way. The old world relied on email, usernames, and passwords; the new world revolves around wallet addresses, private keys, and smart contracts.

Today, entire crypto economies depend on BitGo infrastructure as the vital link between centralized and decentralized finance.

For sophisticated institutions and astute builders, we're unveiling key components in our Fall 2024 release to help catalyze the future of finance.
