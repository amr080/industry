A Turnkey Disbursements Platform

Initiate and disburse bulk payments that move in real-time and settle instantly 24/7/365 with full certainty of funds delivery. Streamline recipient onboarding with seamless wallet creation and tap into global cash-out partners on the Stellar network.

Benefits

Scales In Seconds

Cost Effective Instant Bulk Payments

Move money globally, near instantly, 24/7. Send payments of any size in seconds, with real-time tracking and delivery. Our open-source tools let you scale quickly and disburse funds to thousands in minutes at a fraction of what it costs with traditional payment systems.

Scalability

Batch sends up to 10,000 disbursements in minutes. Simplify onboarding with built-in wallet creation and tap into a global network of over 400,000 off-ramps to cash-out in local currency on Stellar.

Ease of Use

Send global payments starting at just 1 cent. Manage global payouts for anyone - payroll, creators, vendors, and more. Customized deployment options, including self and third-party hosting, giving flexibility as needs scale.

Security

More secure and private payments. Period. This user-friendly solution offers granular access control and minimizes data collection. Designed to prioritize your data security with rigorous audits (200+ hours, no critical vulnerabilities).

Transparency

Track funds in real-time. Send money globally in seconds. Built-in visibility throughout the process.

Senders disburse funds directly to digital wallets created by recipients via SMS invitations to receive funds. When creating a disbursement, the organization selects a specific wallet provider to receive the funds.

There are four user permission levels that enable certain viewing and approval processes for disbursements to ensure data integrity and privacy. An administrator creates additional organization users and can set specific permissions by function.
