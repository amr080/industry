Who We Are

Working to Build the World we Imagine

The Stellar Development Foundation is an organization of innovators, dedicated to building a more accessible world. Were a global, mission-driven team of technologists, scientists, designers, product developers, marketers, and more, working to shape the future of the global financial system. If that inspires you, come join us!

Values

What we value

Be outcome-oriented

We need to play to win. That means setting our sights on the outcome we want and going for it, working collaboratively to define the goal, and committing to driving all our efforts towards success.

Build with urgency

The world will move forward with or without us. We build with urgency to make an impact. We seek pragmatic solutions to difficult problems. We are iterative, learning and reinventing as we go and as we move towards our mission.

Speak with candor and listen with respect

We believe in openness and a culture rooted in feedback without hierarchy. We improve by listening to others, learning from them, and capturing the opportunity to improve. We improve by speaking with candor, offering our insights while valuing and supporting our colleagues.

Benefits

This is what you'll get

  • Competitive health, dental & vision coverage with most plans covered at 100% for the employee + any dependants
  • Gym reimbursement ($80 per month)
  • Wellbeing benefits (One Medical, Rightway, Headspace)
  • Up to 12 weeks of paid parental leave for both non-birthing and birthing parents, as well as up to 14 weeks of paid pregnancy leave for birthing parents
  • Family building benefits through Kindbody
  • Flexible time off + 15 company holidays including a company-wide holiday break
  • Life & ADD (up to $50K)
  • Short & Long term disability
  • 401K with 4% match
  • Health & Dependent Care FSA Accounts
  • Health Savings Account (HSA) with monthly employer contribution
  • L&D budget of $1,500/year
  • Company retreats
  • Commuter benefits with $250/month employer contribution
  • Daily lunch and snacks in office
