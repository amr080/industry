    Description

    Citizen Wallet is designed to be used by citizens as their every day wallet. It is good enough and safe enough for keeping small change, similar to a physical wallet.

    Our goal is to make token exchange work for citizens. Manage multiple accounts, set a user profile, generate vouchers, send and receive tokens.

    Your account is generated, owned and controlled by you. The access key is encrypted and stored on your device's cloud backup. This means that you can easily migrate to a new device and bring your accounts with you while making sure that no one else has access to your accounts.
