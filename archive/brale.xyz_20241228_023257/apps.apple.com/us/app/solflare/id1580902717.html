    Description

    - The first Solana wallet, managing $15B in assets and trusted by 3M traders.

    - Perfect for beginners and pros looking to build wealth on Solana.

    Here's why Solflare is your go-to wallet for Solana:

    - Your security is our number one priority
    From day one, we’ve been focused on keeping your assets safe. With zero security issues to date, our unbreakable security system protects you as you freely explore the Solana ecosystem. You'll have all the tools and peace of mind to fully enjoy all things Solana.

    - Buy crypto with the best rates
    Widest coverage, low fees, and 130+ payment methods! Solflare wallet makes it incredibly easy to buy crypto. You can purchase Solana directly in the app and quickly convert traditional currencies like USD or EUR to crypto with just a few taps.

    - Tap. Confirm. Done.
    Easily send funds to any Solana address or scan a QR code for instant transfers. Choose from recent contacts or your address book for convenience, or share your QR code/wallet address to receive funds quickly.

    - Stake your SOL, earn passive income
    Put your SOL to work by helping secure the Solana network while earning rewards. By staking, you can earn passive income without the need to actively trade. While staked, your SOL is locked, but you can use instant unstake for quick access to your funds when needed.

    - Exchange coins with ease
    Swap Solana coins at the best rates with just a few taps. With full support for all Solana coins, you have more ways to trade smarter. Choose from over 200,000 Solana tokens at any time. As soon as they’re created, you'll be able to trade them.

    - Never miss out on trends
    Explore trends and discover new investment opportunities. Stay ahead with custom watchlists, real-time data, and trends. Easy discovery + Early entry = Better Return on Investment

    - Adjust your portfolio to your needs
    Easily manage and personalize your entire portfolio from a single page. Customize your view to prioritize what matters most to you, whether it’s tracking meme coins, showcasing NFTs, or viewing staking rewards.

    - Profit notifications you can’t ignore
    Stay informed about market moves with automatic and customizable alerts. Receive notifications on price changes and trends when it matters. With timely information, you’ll have more insights to act quickly and make the most of new opportunities.

    - Limit orders, limitless possibilities
    Plan ahead, automate trades, and earn more. Stay on top of your trading game, even when you’re away. With limit orders, you can set your trades in advance and let Solflare do the work. Your tokens will be automatically delivered as soon as the price hits your target.

    - All your favorite Solana apps are just one tap away
    Easily access and interact with your favorite Solana dApps directly from the wallet, including Jupiter, Raydium, Pump.fun, DEX Screener, and Magic Eden.

    - Solflare live chat
    Everyone gets stuck sometimes. If you need assistance, our support team is available around the clock. Whether you're stuck or just have questions, you can easily connect with us through live chat 24/7.

    - Cold wallet support
    Securely connect your hardware wallets like Ledger and Keystone for top-level security. Manage your tokens and NFTs while keeping your assets offline and extra safe.

    - Your personalized NFT gallery
    Solflare makes it easy to store, view, manage, and even instantly sell your Solana NFTs. You can use your collectibles as a profile picture, send them to others, and manage large quantities all at once.

    Install Solflare today for a secure and seamless Solana experience.

    ifeignit , 08/19/2022

    Functional app, seems a bit barren

    While Solflare’s iOS app is functional, it is a bit basic. I wish it had feature parity with comparable wallets on other blockchains. For example, though I can assign nicknames to wallets, they are not easily viewable without going to the account switcher UI, most other wallet apps give you the option to display the nickname in place of the address. Another major letdown is the activity tab. The information there is barely useful, pressing it does not open any detailed view or an explorer like solscan. Honestly activity should have its own dedicated tab and should not be buried under each individual SPL asset. One other major missing feature is an address book. There are several wallets I interact with regularly, I’d love to save their addresses in the app. If I’m being brutally honest, I use Solflare solely for the Ledger support on mobile, at the moment the other major Solana wallet’s does not work with Ledger devices on mobile
