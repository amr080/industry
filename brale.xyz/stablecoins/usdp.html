About USDP

Paxos believes the adoption of Pax Dollar (USDP) will drive greater efficiency and instantaneous transactions for today's institutions and consumers, while also inviting the unbanked to participate in the digital economy in a safe and easy way. USDP is for anyone who wants to store and send money safely, quickly and affordably. Stablecoins have the ability to transform global payments, and USDP can be a trusted mainstream solution. USDP provides financial access to everyone - even those without bank accounts.
