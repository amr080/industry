USDGLO and SBC are now supported at BitGo

2024.02.29
1 min read
Share

We’re happy to announce that starting today, Glo Dollar (USDGLO) and Stable Coin (SBC) are supported by BitGo and available for settlement on the Go Network.

USDGLO

Glo Dollar

SBC

Stable Coin

Stablecoins can now be minted directly by Brale customers to BitGo wallets to make available to businesses in all 50 states and many countries worldwide. BitGo is a qualified custodian with bankruptcy remote trusts in South Dakota, New York, Switzerland, and Germany, serving over 1,500 institutional clients and exchanges in 50 countries.

BitGo offers comprehensive and scalable digital infrastructure which includes hot, custodial, and self-managed cold wallets with flexible transaction signing policies based on volume thresholds, whitelisted wallets, and other controls.

Settle on the Go Network

Through the Go Network, BitGo clients now have the ability to settle USDGLO and SBC across both USD and digital assets securely and in real-time with other Go Network participants, including exchanges and market makers. BitGo’s qualified custody infrastructure with Go Network’s 24/7 settlement rails helps ensure that counterparty risk is greatly reduced in the digital asset marketplace.

You can find Brale in the public directory of contacts to open a buy or sell order of USDGLO and SBC.

We’re excited to work with BitGo to increase access to Brale-issued stablecoins. Businesses with BitGo accounts can get started by minting or depositing USDGLO or SBC to BitGo wallets.
