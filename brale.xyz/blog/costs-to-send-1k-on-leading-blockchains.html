Stablecoins are 5X-1000X cheaper than traditional payment methods

Stablecoin payments on all leading L1 and L2 blockchains surpass traditional payment method fees, settle nearly instantly, and benefit from 24/7 always on, global operations.

Almost all supported chains offer sub $0.01 transaction fees up to 100X cheaper than ACH, with stablecoin payments on Stellar, Celo, Hedera, and Solana approaching the cost of relational database updates.

The costs of stablecoin payments are low enough that the treasury economics of a stablecoin program can effectively subsidize programs on Base, Solana, Stellar, and many blockchains.
