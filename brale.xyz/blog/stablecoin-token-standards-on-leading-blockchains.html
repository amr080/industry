Blockchain token standards vary in their design and functionality. Some, like the ERC-20 standard, provide a common interface that offers great flexibility but generally requires more effort to implement. Others, like Solana's SPL Token Standard, integrate the token implementation into the blockchain itself, simplifying the issuance process while introducing certain limitations.

Bringing native stablecoins to emerging ecosystems

We've had the opportunity to speak with many emerging ecosystems about bringing native stablecoins to their network. The time to implement an Ethereum or Solana-compatible blockchain is less than a week, and typically a month to implement other standards.

Once implemented, stablecoins have onramps and offramps built-in, and leverage the same technology, treasury management, and transparency backing stablecoins on 11 blockchains today.

We're excited to partner with emerging ecosystems to increase access to stablecoins. To learn more, please reach out to our team.
