Introducing Brale Business Accounts

2024.07.25
5 min read
Share

Business accounts are a simple way for businesses around the country to use popular stablecoins without the challenges of tying together multiple solutions.

Business accounts boast an easy UI for managing stablecoins across blockchains and a familiar interface for depositing and withdrawing USD to your bank account. They also abstract all of the complexity of using blockchains and there are no pre-funding accounts for gas fees or any of that stress.

Business accounts have everything you'd expect with incredible pricing to match.

Generate cards for expenditures with fine-tuned controls

Thanks to our partnership with Rain, you can generate corporate cards for your business and spend your stablecoins anywhere VISA is accepted. Rain cards support Brale-issued (and non-Brale-issued) stablecoins natively. Generate spendable cards in the Rain UI.
