Announcing support for USDC on Base, Arbitrum, and Celo

2024.08.01
2 min read
Share

We’re excited to announce that Brale Business accounts now support native USDC on Base, Arbitrum, and Celo. Business accounts support free USDC onramping, offramping, and 1:1 swaps with Brale-issued stablecoins. This extends account support alongside USDC on Ethereum, Polygon, Avalanche, and Optimism, and USDP on Solana.
