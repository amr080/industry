October conferences for Brale

2024.10.08
4 min read
Share

For the first time in Brale history, we're making a concerted effort to be at several conferences in October. Across the team, we'll be in Salt Lake City, Las Vegas, San Francisco, Los Angeles, and London throughout the month.

Below is a guide to where we will be, who is attending, and how to get a meeting. We hope to gather interest and partnerships as part of our last BETA group of clients and partners before a fully open release.

If you've been stablecoin curious, are interested in bringing native stablecoins to your app, emerging ecosystem, or embed them in your payment flows, we'd love to help.

On the Precipice of Decentralization

Sun, Oct 27
12:35 - 1:00 PM PT
Add to calendar

Fintech has been slowly deconstructing the analog past of legacy tech stacks, utilizing first principles to undo certain systems. We are at the dawn of decentralizing tools having the capacity and speed to truly decentralize financial services. Through tokenization, and the immediacy of stablecoins, bankers will have to rethink the entire nature of they intend to move money. This panel will paint the vision for a future that is starting right now.
