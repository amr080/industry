Money is deeply personal. Yet, it’s a topic that most people only understand on the surface level. Beyond that opaque surface, there is a hidden world of sophisticated financial strategies, gate kept behind the walls of institutional banks and only accessible through wealth managers.

With modern day technology, financial control is shifting to the hands of everyday investors like you. Frec exists to enable that shift and give everyone the chance to create new opportunities for their wealth.

About Frec

Frec was founded in 2021 and is based out of San Francisco. We’re funded by a long list of incredible investors from companies like Google, Meta, Brex, and more. Our biggest backer is Greylock. Frec has two subsidiaries: one is an SEC registered investment adviser and the other is an SEC registered broker dealer and FINRA member. Assets are housed at Apex Clearing House.
