FAQ

The initial investment minimum to start a Frec Direct Indexing portfolio for most indices is $20,000. For indices that have a greater number of positions, the minimum is $50,000. This is the minimum starting amount in order to purchase almost every stock in the index, resulting in more accurate tracking of the index.

Frec does not charge any trading fees for stocks and ETFs on top of the regulatory fees charged by financial regulators, which are typically a very small amount per trade. Read more about regulatory trading fees here. However, Frec customers can buy or sell mutual funds on the platform, and for some of those mutual funds, there is a $20 fee per trade.

Our current interest rate for our portfolio line of credit is subject to change based on the Effective Federal Funds Rate. Interest is billed monthly and is automatically added to your loan balance. Unlike most traditional loans, you aren’t required to make monthly principal payments for a portfolio line of credit. You may need to make payments in case of margin calls, but we can help you forecast if, when, and how much you’d owe. Learn more about margin calls here
