SkyLink x LayerZero: Unlocking Distribution for Real World Assets through Yield Streaming
November 14, 2024

Today, we're thrilled to announce that Plume SkyLink has integrated with LayerZero, enabling a seamless and secure way to abstract yield distribution for real-world assets (RWAs) across multiple blockchain ecosystems. This partnership brings Plume's vision of a connected RWAfi landscape to life, allowing investors to access and manage institutional-grade yields from anywhere permissionlessly.

What is SkyLink?

SkyLink is our proprietary solution for secure, cross-chain RWA distribution. Designed as a solution for interoperable RWAs, SkyLink allows investors on different chains to receive real-time yield directly to their wallets. SkyLink redefines how cross-chain interactions happen for tokenized assets, enabling users to track and access yield confidently across any number of applications they choose within any ecosystem.

Key features of Plume SkyLink:

  • RWA Interoperability: Seamlessly access RWA yields across chains while preserving Plume's asset custody and compliance infrastructure, reducing risk and enhancing efficiency.
  • Real-time Yield Streaming: Yield is distributed continuously, empowering investors to enjoy instant access and redemption.
  • Distribution Hub: SkyLink allows the distribution of RWAs across different networks beyond gated environments while removing traditional friction points to enhance accessibility.
