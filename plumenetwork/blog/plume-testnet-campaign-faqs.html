Plume Testnet Campaign FAQs
July 10, 2024

Welcome to the Plume Testnet Campaign FAQ! Here, we address all your pressing questions. Whether you’re eager to learn how to get an invite code or accumulate more Plume Miles, we have the information you need.

What is the official Plume’s Testnet Link?

The official Plume Testnet can be accessed at https://miles.plumenetwork.xyz/. This is the central hub for interacting with Plume’s test environment, where you can participate in various testnet activities.

How long is the Testnet running for?

The Testnet will be available for an extended period, giving you ample time to interact and explore its features. This is not its final form; we will continuously add new elements and improvements over the coming weeks to enhance your experience. Stay engaged to see all the exciting updates!

Is goonUSD a real stablecoin?

No, goonUSD is a test stablecoin specifically designed for the Plume Network’s testnet environment. It is not a real stablecoin and can’t be used for actual financial transactions. Its primary purpose is to facilitate testing and development within the Plume ecosystem.

How do I add $GOON & $goonUSD to my wallet?

To add $GOON and $goonUSD to your wallet, you generally need to add them as custom tokens. Here is a brief guide:

  1. Open your wallet and navigate to the “Add Token” or “Custom Token” section.
  2. Enter the contract address for the token you want to add.
    - GOON: 0xbA22114ec75f0D55C34A5E5A3cf384484Ad9e733
    - goonUSD: 0x5c1409a46cD113b3A667Db6dF0a8D7bE37ed3BB3
  3. Your wallet should automatically fill in the token symbol and decimals. If not, you may need to enter these details manually.
  4. Confirm and save the token.

Each wallet interface might differ slightly, so we recommend doing your own research on the specific steps for the wallet you are using.

Does holding more testnet ETH or GOON in my wallet ensure more rewards in the future?

No, claiming multiple times from the faucet will not increase your miles nor guarantee more rewards in future campaigns.

How do I know when 24 hours have passed to claim miles again from recurring tasks?

It’s very easy! These tasks reset at the same time as the check-in. We suggest completing all tasks at once every day to maintain your streak and maximize your miles.

How many times can I complete the recurring tasks?

As many times as you want! We built the testnet for you to enjoy and experiment with. However, please note that only the first transaction you make at the beginning of the testnet period will earn you miles. More transactions per day do not mean more miles.

My Testnet transaction failed. What should I do?

If your transaction on the Testnet fails, report it to us immediately with the transaction ID. Please open a ticket on our Discord server and provide a detailed explanation of the issue, including screenshots. This helps us identify and resolve any problems promptly.

I think something is wrong with the Testnet, faucet, etc. What should I do?

Remember that we are in a stage of construction, feedback, and improvement, so sometimes we have more goons than expected, and the systems need updates! For this reason, we created this status page where you can verify if it is a general problem or if it is about your account in particular, so before getting alarmed, it would be best to first visit this page: https://status.plumenetwork.xyz/

Will there be rewards for on chain activity?

This campaign is about testing our product and understanding how our community interacts. While we don’t have specific rewards promised for every on chain activity right now, we deeply appreciate your participation and contributions. Plume values its community immensely, and we aim to reward every effort you make. So, while we can’t guarantee specific sums today, rest assured that your interactions with the testnet will be recognized and rewarded in meaningful ways. Thank you for being a part of our journey!

Which wallets do we recommend for navigating the Plume Testnet?

For the best experience on the Plume Testnet, we recommend using the Chrome browser and the Metamask wallet extension. Metamask is widely supported and provides a seamless experience for interacting with blockchain applications. If you encounter issues with other wallets, please report them to us with detailed information so we can address compatibility and provide support.

How can I add the Plume Testnet chain to my wallet?

The Testnet page is optimized to connect you directly when you start your interactions. However, if you prefer to connect in other ways, please check our documentation for detailed instructions: https://docs.plumenetwork.xyz/plume/getting-started/connecting-to-plume.

What is the utility of the passport?

The passport serves as your identification within the testnet campaign, keeping a record of all the activities you have completed on the Plume platform. Each stamp represents a completed activity. Additionally, the passport tracks your Miles and referral numbers.

Upgrading your passport category enhances your ranking within the community, making it easier to earn more Miles and access additional perks.

How can I earn more Miles?

You can participate in various activities and tasks on the Plume Network to earn more Plume Miles. Here are some ways to accumulate Miles:

  • Complete tasks listed on the “Earn Miles” page.
  • Engage in referral programs and invite others to join the Plume Network.
  • Participate in daily check-ins to earn consistent rewards.

What are the stamps?

Stamps are collectible items awarded for completing specific tasks within the Plume Network. They serve as a way to track your progress and achievements. Each task you complete will earn you a stamp, which can be viewed in your passport. Collecting stamps may unlock additional rewards and recognition within the community.

Why are my Glaxe tasks not being verified?

There can be several reasons for this issue. A common problem is not using the same wallet on both platforms. Another frequent issue is using the Plume Wallet as a secondary address in Galxe, which will not verify your tasks. To ensure proper verification, please use your wallet as the MAIN wallet in Galxe. Otherwise, your tasks won’t be verified.

If you’re already experiencing issues due to using a secondary wallet, it’s best to remove the secondary wallet from your Galxe account. Then, create a new Galxe account with this wallet set as the primary one. This should resolve the verification problem.

How do I earn a reroll?

You can earn a reroll by checking in for five consecutive days. This consistent engagement rewards you with the opportunity to exchange your current Plume Goon NFT for a different one, adding an element of excitement and variety to your collection.

Rerolls mean I can get +2 NFTs in my wallet?

No, rerolling does not mean you will get additional NFTs. If you choose to reroll, you will replace your current Plume Goon NFT with a different one. This feature allows you to try to obtain a different Plume Goon, but it does not increase the total number of NFTs in your wallet.

What time do we need to check in daily?

Daily check-ins are reset every 24 hours at 00:00 UTC. Make sure to check in within this window to maintain your streak and earn rewards.

How many points can I earn with the faucet?

You can use the faucet every 10 minutes to earn points. However, there is a daily cap of 5,000 Miles that you can accumulate through faucet interactions. This limit ensures fair distribution of rewards.

Why Ambient won’t let me interact with the app because of my location?

For regulatory reasons beyond the control of Plume Network, Ambient has restricted operations in certain countries. At Plume, we strive to be an open network for the entire community, so the rest of our activities are available to you. We regret any inconvenience this may cause.

The restricted countries are: Afghanistan, Serbia, North Macedonia, Belarus, Burma, Central African Republic, Cuba, Democratic Republic of Congo, Ethiopia, Iran, Iraq, Lebanon, Libya, Mali, Nicaragua, North Korea, Russia, Somalia, South Sudan, Sudan, Syria, Venezuela, Yemen, Zimbabwe.

How can I get an invite code?

To obtain an invite code, join our Discord channel and request one in the #invite-codes section. Our community members and moderators are there to help you get started with the Plume Network.

How do I mint a Plume Goon if I missed the early access period?

If you missed the early access period, all 1 million Plume Goon NFTs have now been sold out. We appreciate your interest and support.

What perks does each of the flight classes get?

We want this to be a surprise that you will discover as you upgrade your passport so that we won’t spoil all the details. However, we can tell you that the rewards include multipliers for your Miles, enhanced referral benefits, and even better rewards. So, don’t forget to upgrade whenever possible to unlock these exciting perks!

What happened with the boarding groups?

We couldn’t wait for everyone to have access to the testnet! So, we decided to open the doors for all users to come and try what we prepared for you.

In addition to the Miles you accumulated during the last few weeks, you will receive extra Miles based on the boarding group you belonged to at the time of the testnet launch. This way, everyone gets a head start and can fully enjoy the testnet experience.
