Introducing Plume Passport: Your RWAfi Wallet
January 10, 2025

Plume Passport—our forthcoming smart wallet solution—embodies the core mission of Plume: bridging real world assets (RWAs) with the onchain crypto world. It is your foot in the door to access all key aspects of Plume’s ecosystem, all the way from asset tokenization, RWA yield opportunities, governance and much more! While it’s not yet live, we’re thrilled to unveil the Plume Passport landing page and invite you to explore what’s on the horizon.

‍

Plume Passport’s key focus is simplicity and compliance: native account abstraction ensures truly gasless transactions while enabling you to maintain complete control of your wallet. 

‍

Coupled with Plume’s built-in identity and compliance layers, it turns real world asset management from a regulatory headache into a straightforward process. Instead of simply holding tokenized assets, you’ll soon be able to stake them, use them as collateral, and seamlessly integrate with Plume’s thriving RWAfi ecosystem—all from one user-friendly interface.

‍

At the heart of Plume Passport is a robust architecture designed to enhance composability - access a holistic view of your portfolio, including details on yield tokens and asset tokens that other wallets can’t easily display. This integrated approach breaks down the barriers that have historically prevented real world assets from reaching their full potential on-chain.

‍

Currently undergoing rigorous audits, Plume Passport is slated for release as soon as we’re confident it meets the highest security and performance standards. In the meantime, head over to our brand-new landing page to learn more and join the waitlist. By signing up for early access, you’ll be among the first to explore advanced RWA staking, collateralization, and DeFi in a wallet environment built specifically for the future of RWAfi. We can’t wait to see how you’ll leverage Plume Passport to pioneer the next era of real-world asset finance.

‍

Sign up here: https://forms.plumenetwork.xyz/passport
