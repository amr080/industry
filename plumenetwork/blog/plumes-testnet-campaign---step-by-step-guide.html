Plume’s Testnet Campaign - Step by Step Guide
July 13, 2024

We have launched Season 1 of our Testnet Campaign, designed to engage and reward ourcommunity members while also providing new users a platform to learn more about RWAs. Woohoo!

Here is your journey:

  • Our story starts with you as the Plume Goon, who will travel around the world over the course of the campaign, visiting different countries to represent our partners and projects.
  • When you sign up for Plume’s campaign, you will receive your own invite codes and Plume Goon passports, where you can start collecting stamps from countries you visit.
  • Active participants will be awarded Plume Points throughout the campaign via various tasks, including completing quests along the journey, referring friends with invite codes, and so on.

From understanding how to earn points to leveraging the leaderboard and passport features, we’ve covered all the essential aspects to ensure you have a seamless and rewarding journey within the Plume community. This article is a step-by-step guide that walks you through the various campaign features.

So, let’s dive in and explore everything our Testnet Campaign has to offer. Welcome to the Goon Squad.

Pre-Boarding Phase

You might want to mint your goon before boarding, so check here and follow the steps. If you already have a Goon, proceed directly to the next phase!

Boarding Phase

Upon completing the pre-boarding phase, users move onto the onboarding phase, which is a phase where they perform activities related to testnet.

Earn Miles

  1. On the “Earn Miles” page, users can perform these tasks and earn more miles.
  2. To earn more Miles, you have to complete the following social tasks:
  • Follow Plume on X: You will automatically follow @PlumeNetwork on X
  • Join Discord
  • Drip from Faucet: Earn 5000 miles when you use the faucet. You can use the faucet more than once per day, but you only get max 5000 points per day.
  • And so on… join the testnet to find it out yourself!

Check In

1) In the Earn Miles page, go to “Check in”

2) Upon checking in, you will see that day that are ❌ means you failed to check in. ✔️ means that you have successfully checked in.

You can “check-in” once per day here

The next day reset at 00:00 UTC

If you check in 5 times in a row, you get a re-roll for a new Plume Goon NFT. Try it out soon!

Faucet

1) At the Earn Miles page, go to “Faucet.”

2) Claim testnet ETH and Goon Testnet Tokens, and you will see this pop-up message that says you will receive it within a minute.

Passport

You can check how many stamps you’ve collected with your Passport.

1) On the Earn Miles page, go to “Passport,” or users can access it via the tab in the menu bar.

2) Check your ranking, and you can upgrade your status as you earn more miles by referring more friends. You get more re-rolls for a new Plume Goon NFT if your flight class is higher.

LFGOON!

The Plume Network’s Season 1 Testnet Campaign is your ticket to an exciting adventure! As a Plume Goon, you’ll explore RWAs, earn miles, collect stamps, and climb the leaderboard through fun and engaging tasks. From onboarding to earning miles and checking your passport, each step gets you closer to awesome rewards and higher statuses.

Welcome to the Goon Squad — let’s make this adventure unforgettable!
