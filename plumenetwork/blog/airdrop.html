Introducing Plume’s Airdrop
January 14, 2025

Register now for Plume’s Airdrop Season 1 to mark the beginning of a transformative journey.

We’re incredibly excited to announce the first step in our path towards decentralization with the inaugural season of the Plume Airdrop. Our mission at Plume is to build a new world onchain that unifies the existing onchain economy and the real world. By building this bridge we can create a unified world where interacting with real world assets is as easy as interacting with crypto native assets – completely open, permissionless, and composable. 

Plume has always been community first. We’ve been incredibly fortunate to get to know, work with, and build with so many incredible people from all over the world. Our ethos is to build a user centric world where individuals and communities have control – not middlemen and rent seekers. And as we’re building this world together, it’s only natural that we launch Plume hand in hand with our community. This airdrop is the first step. 

As we prepare for Plume’s launch, this airdrop is intended to distribute tokens to participants in our ecosystem. While this airdrop is focused on early supporters, future initiatives will allocate significant resources for the Plume builder community. Together, we will build a new era of crypto where our purpose-built RWA chain and RWAfi ecosystem unlocks new use cases and creates a unified global economy accessible to the next billion users, dollars, and assets.

Recognizing Contributors

Plume’s Season 1 Airdrop is designed to reward key contributors across the Plume ecosystem. The allocation of the airdrop reflects the importance of participation, engagement, and shared vision.

Both Early Adopters and Global Community Members are required to fill out the registration form to ensure eligibility for the airdrop claim.

Early Adopters

The dedication of our early adopters and the strength of our community have been instrumental in Plume's development and growth.

Testnet users engaged in extensive hands-on testing, generating over 18 million wallets and executing more than 280 million transactions. This active participation allowed for rigorous stress-testing and valuable feedback on network performance and usability.

Pre-deposit stakers provided financial backing through the StakeStone and Nest campaigns, demonstrating a strong belief in Plume's potential to revolutionize RWAfi. Their participation is a signal of the health and potential of Plume as a highly robust and liquid chain.

Contributors that have been paramount to Plume's growth have been segmented into three groups, representing all types of participants and their unique contributions to the success of Plume. 

Testnet Users with Miles: Plume’s testnet saw over 18M wallets and 280M transactions in 2024. Rewards will be distributed based on Plume Miles, Stamps earned, and all airplane mode tasks. To protect genuine users that have put time and effort into optimizing the network, there will be a set of anti-sybil measures in place to ensure fairness. 

Active Community Members: Holders of select roles in the Plume Discord server who participated in the testnet campaign are eligible for a share in the airdrop. These roles were given out to early community members and active participants in the Plume Discord. Social Miners in the Plume Goons Circle and select Telegram participants are also eligible for airdrop rewards.

Pre-deposit Stakers: Stakestone and Nest campaigns raised over $64M in 2024. Users who deposited $100 or more and held their funds in the vaults until TGE, will receive rewards proportional to their deposits.

Global Community Members

Plume’s Season 1 Airdrop is also intended to reward key communities in both the RWA ecosystem as well as the Plume ecosystem.

Pioneers like Ondo have built a foundation for both crypto natives and institutions to gather around. Polymarket has helped expand the story around RWAs away from just things like security tokens into how we see it – making it easy for users to interact with the real world onchain. AIXBT embodies how we see the future – where crypto, the real world, and AI all collapse into a single plane and live together side by side. Backed, Dinari, Swarm X and their users have all innovated in RWAs in novel ways. And we also support the incoming administration’s efforts towards a more collaborative and open stance regarding crypto in America and its initiatives such as World Liberty.

By including these communities, Plume aims to reward those who we have worked with, those we have learned from, and also foster a shared sense of ownership to encourage active participation in our RWAfi ecosystem. 

The airdrop also serves as an invitation for these communities to explore and participate in Plume’s robust suite of projects and tools, fostering synergy and cross-pollination within the broader crypto landscape.

  • Polymarket
  • Ondo
  • World Liberty Financial (WLFI)
  • AIXBT by Virtuals
  • Select users of Backed Assets, Dinari, and Swarm X

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  1. How do I register for the airdrop, and what information is required?
    Registering is easy! Just fill out the form with your wallet address and some basic info:
    https://registration.plumenetwork.xyz
    ‍
  2. What happens if I miss the registration deadline?
    Missing the deadline means ineligibility for the Season 1 airdrop. So, make sure to register on time! If you do miss the deadline, you can join Season 2, and prior activity from the testnet will be accounted for.
    ‍
  3. How can I check my allocation?
    You must first register, and then shortly before TGE, you’ll be able to see your allocation and claim. This page will be updated to inform the community when that happens. Please do not use any unofficial website to check your allocation or claim as it will be a scam.
    ‍
  4. I have miles, but I was not eligible for this airdrop. What will happen to my miles?
    In case you missed season 1 claiming, your miles will carry over into Season 2. We will continue to reward our community for active engagement and participation on the network.
    ‍
  5. When will the airdrop be distributed?
    After the claim period starts. For onchain claims, distribution occurs at TGE. For exchange (CEX) claims, it's after exchange claim approval.
    ‍
  6. How do I claim my Plume Season 1 Airdrop?
    After you register, please wait until we announce claims and allocations, after which you'll have two options:
    - Claim now: Get some tokens right away and the rest later.
    - Delayed claim: Get your tokens later, but with a boost on your allocation.
    The time to receive the tokens can be reduced with onchain activity on Plume’s RWA Chain.
    ‍
  7. How is my allocation calculated?
    An individual's share of the airdrop is calculated based on eligibility criteria, contributions, activity (like testnet "miles"), and measures to ensure fair distribution (anti-sybil measures).
    ‍
  8. How can I check if I’m eligible for the airdrop?
    An eligibility and allocation checker is coming soon! Keep an eye on Plume Foundation's official Twitter for the announcement.
    ‍
  9. Can I claim from multiple categories?
    Yes, if eligible for multiple categories, claims are consolidated.
    ‍
  10. What are the onchain claim gas fees?
    Onchain gas fees depend on network conditions and traffic. Monitoring gas prices during the claim window is recommended.
    ‍
  11. Which exchanges will be supported after TGE?
    The list of supporting exchanges will be announced closer to the TGE date.
    ‍
  12. What is the process and deadline for claiming on exchanges?
    The exchange claim form, including wallet and exchange details, must be submitted. The deadline will be shared here later.
    ‍
  13. What is the token allocation for the Plume Season 1 Airdrop?
    Plume will share tokenomics details soon. Stay tuned.
    ‍
  14. Will there be guidance for future airdrops and eligibility?
    Yes, details about future airdrops will be shared after the mainnet launch.
    ‍
  15. What about taxes?
    Tax implications depend on individual circumstances and jurisdiction. It's recommended to consult a tax advisor for personalized guidance.
    ‍
  16. I’m having issues with the registration form! Where can I get help?
    Please reach out to our community managers in the #support channel in Discord.
