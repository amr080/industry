Berlin-based Swarm Markets has launched a fully asset-backed digital gold represented by non-fungible tokens (NFTs). 2）RWA Market Commentary

Key Highlights:

Launch of Tokenized Gold by Swarm Markets: The Berlin-based platform Swarm Markets has made a notable entry into the RWA space by launching tokenized gold backed by non-fungible tokens (NFTs). Each NFT corresponds to a specific gold bar, verified by unique identifiers and securely stored in Brink’s vaults in London. This initiative is set to enhance the liquidity and accessibility of gold trading, attracting a broader range of investors.

Institutional Interest and Market Growth: McKinsey & Company’s forecast of a $2 trillion market cap for tokenized RWAs by 2030, with bullish scenarios projecting up to $4 trillion, highlights the immense growth potential of this sector. Similarly, Boston Consulting Group (BCG) and 21Shares predict market caps of $16 trillion and $10 trillion, respectively. Such projections are bolstered by the involvement of leading financial institutions like BlackRock, Fidelity, and JPMorgan, indicating a significant shift towards blockchain technology in traditional finance.
