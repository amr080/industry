RWAfi Ecosystem Spotlight 🪶 (October 25 — November 1)
October 25, 2024

This week, we’re highlighting some of the top projects building on Plume Network, each playing a key role in shaping the future of Real World Asset Finance (RWAfi). These partnerships demonstrate how Plume’s flexible infrastructure supports tokenizing real world assets, enhancing scalability and accessibility. Keep up with the latest ecosystem news by following us on Twitter!

Dinari

About Dinari
Dinari issues dShares, 1:1 backed tokenized stocks, ETFs, REITs, and other Real World Assets (RWAs). Dinari holds reserves of each underlying asset in audited vaults as a US company and SEC-registered transfer agent. With $10 million in seed funding from top investors like 500 Global, Susquehanna SIG, and Alchemy, Dinari is making waves in the tokenized asset space. Investors can access dShares and yield-bearing stablecoin USD+ at Dinari’s dedicated platforms. Developers can learn more about the API at dinari.com/business.

Significant Achievements & Milestones

  • dShares issued: 50+ stocks, ETFs, and REITs
  • Total Value Locked: $11.53M
  • Transactions Filled: 3,000 orders
  • Live Chains: Ethereum Mainnet, Arbitrum, Blast, Base, Kinto, Plume Testnet
  • Integrated B2B Partners: 20+ across various regions
  • Grants: Multiple Arbitrum grants
  • Social Stats: Twitter growth to 147K followers

How do Dinari and Plume work together?
Dinari is bringing assets, including public equities (stocks, ETFs, etc) and stablecoin utility, to Plume Network, as well as a number of other initiatives we’ll be looking forward to announcing in the future.

Ongoing Campaigns
Dinari recently launched the FinTech Developer Incentive Program and Business Start Program. These initiatives simplify tokenized asset integration for FinTechs, early-stage businesses, and startups.

Key Benefits:

  • Earn up to $200 per developer when integrating the Dinari API.
  • Receive $10,000 in trading credits for a full year.
  • Flexible API integration into your app or platform.
  • Exclusive premium support and guidance from our team
  • Coming soon: Earn dShares, $ARB, profit-sharing opportunities by referring partners and growing the Dinari ecosystem with Community Referral Program

RealtyX

About RealtyX

RealtyX is an innovative real estate RWA launchpad, tokenizing properties and transforming them into tradable RWA tokens through its DAO. RealtyX allows users to integrate real estate assets into DeFi, unlocking liquidity for staking, lending, and other financial activities.

RealtyX’s RWA token, RST, provides stablecoin yield and unlocks liquidity, allowing users to leverage real estate for staking, lending, and other DeFi activities in a more accessible and efficient way.

Significant Achievements & Milestones

  • WOW Summit Startup Competition: 2nd place for innovation in real estate tokenization
  • Aptos Accelerator Alumni: Selected from over 120 projects
  • Cyberport Incubation Program: Recognized as part of Hong Kong’s fintech incubation initiative
  • Tokenized First Property: Launched tokenized real estate, enabling RST holders to earn stablecoin yields
  • Utility Vault: Released for third-party Web3 integrations
  • Social Stats: Twitter (66K), Discord (50K), Telegram (11.5K)

How do RealtyX and Plume Work Together?
RealtyX relies on Plume Network’s modular architecture for faster and more scalable transactions, specifically optimized for RWA tokenization. With Plume’s infrastructure, RealtyX ensures frictionless asset trading, staking, and integration across DeFi applications. Both platforms emphasize compliance and scalability, making tokenized real estate accessible on a global scale.

Ongoing Campaigns
RealtyX’s Loyalty Point System on the TaskOn platform rewards active participation, with earned points increasing chances for airdrops of RX governance tokens. An Ambassador Program is set to launch soon, offering community members the opportunity to contribute to RealtyX’s growth while earning rewards in RX.

Credible Finance

About Credible Finance
Credible Finance is revolutionizing decentralized finance (DeFi) by offering tokenized private debt, private equities, and stocks on Solana, backed by renowned investors such as OVioHQ. With a focus on providing liquidity without forfeiting ownership, Credible enables borrowers to leverage tokenized RWAs as collateral, making finance more accessible, transparent, and efficient.

Significant Achievements & Milestones

  • Collateralization of Tokenized RWAs: Up to 75% Loan-to-Value (LTV)
  • Partnerships: Collaboration with trusted tokenization partners like Ondo and Homebase
  • Flexible Borrowing Options: Offering perpetual loans and various terms to suit borrower needs
  • CRED Token Ecosystem: Integrated staking rewards, loyalty shares, and airdrops
  • Seamless Onboarding: Permissionless onboarding for borrowers
  • Litepaper: Credible Litepaper

How do Credible Finance and Plume work together?
Credible Finance utilizes Plume Network’s infrastructure to bring RWAs onchain, making tokenization and management more efficient. This partnership allows Credible to offer quicker, more scalable asset solutions, providing users with easy and compliant access.

Ongoing Campaigns
Credible recently introduced its Credible Points system, rewarding community members with daily airdrops for participating in social activities, governance, staking, and NFT collections. Points are tracked via a leaderboard, and top earners are eligible for generous Solana and Bonk token airdrops, with upcoming rewards in CRED tokens.
