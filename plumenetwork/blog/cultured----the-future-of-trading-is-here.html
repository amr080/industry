Cultured — The Future of Trading is Here
September 10, 2024

We’re excited to bring Cultured to the crypto world! Cultured makes it easy for anyone to express any of their ideas in a financial way—whether it’s speculating on the price of Pokemon cards, sneakers, politics, manga, pop culture, or anything else. Cultured is the fastest way to speculate on anything in the real world.

Currently, live on our testnet, Cultured takes an innovative approach to trade in the real world. We utilize a variety of partners and proprietary data sets to bring in real world data, combine them in interesting ways, and create unique feeds only available on Plume to make it easy for anyone to take positions on any idea they might have, no matter how small or esoteric.

Why Cultured?

Cultured is about combining crypto with the real world. We bring real things onchain and make it easy for anyone to participate. By doing this, we increase the surface area in which crypto can operate and open up a new path for people to come in and trade their favorite topics.

How It Works

By integrating real world data into Cultured across multiple sectors, Cultured enables you to speculate on where things are going in the easiest way possible. Think something is going up? Open a position. Think it’s going down? Short. Really think it’s going down? Throw on some leverage!

Cultured’s simple yet powerful interface provides the tools and insights you need to make your decision for any kind of user, whether you’re a seasoned trader or someone looking to explore the markets for the first time.

Get ready to trade anything from crypto to culture — your beliefs, your markets, your trades. Cultured is coming soon.

About Plume

Plume is the first fully integrated and modular chain focused on RWAfi. We’ve built a composable, scalable ecosystem that supports the seamless onboarding of all types of real world assets, from equities to private credit and beyond. With our end-to-end tokenization engine, over 170 projects are building on Plume’s infrastructure, simplifying compliance and capital management to help projects scale globally.
