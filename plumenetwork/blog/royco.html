Plume Network Partners with Royco Protocol to Enhance RWAfi Liquidity
January 7, 2025

At Plume Network, our #1 mission is to empower real world asset finance (RWAfi) by making it easier for builders and users alike to participate onchain. We’ve simplified the process of asset tokenization and built an accessible ecosystem for users to find and engage with financial tools. 

Now, Plume Network is working with Royco Protocol, the trailblazers of the Incentivized Action Market (IAM) Protocol, to ensure applications within our ecosystem have a viable path to the liquidity they need to thrive from Day One. 

‍

Introducing Royco on Plume

Royco is a protocol designed to create markets for liquidity acquisition. By enabling applications to negotiate directly with liquidity providers (LPs), Royco changes the way RWAfi applications onboard liquidity onto Plume. Whether it’s pre-launch or post-deployment, Royco empowers applications to secure liquidity efficiently and transparently.

Here’s how it works:

  1. Guaranteed Day One Liquidity: Applications create liquidity markets on Royco, specifying incentives (tokens, points, vested assets) and terms. LPs can propose or accept deals, locking their liquidity for predetermined periods.
  2. Direct Incentives: Unlike traditional approaches that require double incentivization (bridging assets, then moving them to applications), Royco allows applications to incentivize liquidity directly into their smart contracts.
  3. Collaborative Liquidity: Applications can co-incentivize markets, reducing competition for shared liquidity and enabling multi-platform yield stacking opportunities.

‍

Reimagining Liquidity for RWAfi

By integrating Royco, Plume Network is introducing a modern, scalable solution to address liquidity challenges for tokenized real-world assets. Here’s what this means for our ecosystem:

  • Transparency: Users and applications gain clarity on terms, durations, and expected returns for liquidity commitments.
  • Trustless Infrastructure: Smart contracts replace centralized counterparts for custody, vesting, and deployment, reducing risk for all parties.
  • Accessibility: LPs can participate from multiple chains, depositing assets like USDC, USDT, or wETH directly into Plume applications.

For applications, this means less time chasing liquidity and more time focusing on product-market fit and scaling their offerings. For LPs, this means clear, actionable opportunities to deploy capital across projects with minimized risk.

‍

Plume x Royco: Building a Better Future for RWAfi

Royco’s deployment on Plume Network represents a leap forward for how liquidity is created and deployed in RWAfi. This partnership ensures that applications launching on Plume have the tools they need to succeed, while users benefit from a more transparent and collaborative financial ecosystem.

Together, Plume and Royco are reimagining what’s possible in Real-World Asset Finance. Stay tuned for updates as we roll out the first Royco-powered applications on Plume!

‍

About Royco

Royco is a platform to optimize incentive spend by enabling onchain negotiation between market creators and LPs. Learn more about Royco at royco.org.

About Plume Network

Plume is the first public L1 blockchain purpose-built for RWAfi, enabling the rapid adoption and demand-driven integration of real world assets. With 180+ projects building on the network, Plume offers a composable, EVM-compatible environment for onboarding and managing diverse real-world assets. Coupled with an end-to-end tokenization engine and a network of financial infrastructure partners, Plume simplifies asset onboarding and enables seamless DeFi integration for RWAs so anyone can tokenize real world assets, distribute them globally, and make them useful for native crypto users.
