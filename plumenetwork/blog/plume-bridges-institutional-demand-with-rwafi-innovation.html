Plume Bridges Institutional Demand with RWAfi Innovation
October 25, 2024

It’s no secret that Real World Assets (RWA) are gaining momentum in tokenization, but let’s talk about how Plume is positioning itself as a major player. With the leadership of our CEO, Chris Yin, we’re driving the shift toward a more liquid and accessible RWA market. While the broader market searches for the right product-market fit, Plume is laser-focused on tapping into the institutional side.

The RWA Happy Hour hosted by Plume brought together influential names reshaping Real World Asset Finance (RWAfi) at the RWA Summit. With WisdomTree, Arbitrum, and Centrifuge, the event was packed with powerhouse institutions advancing blockchain technology’s integration into traditional finance.

WisdomTree, managing over $113B in assets, took center stage, showcasing its blockchain-backed approach to transparency and accessibility in financial products. Arbitrum added to the discussions, presenting its innovative rollup tech for Ethereum scaling, while Centrifuge shared insights into bridging off-chain assets to DeFi.

Chris Yin and Plume led the way, fostering conversations on modular chain integration, institutional liquidity, and strategic partnerships — putting RWAfi in focus for some of the largest stakeholders in finance and DeFi. This was an influential gathering of visionaries driving the future of onchain assets.

Here are some key discussion points about Chris’ strategic direction during RWA summit and RWA happy hour.

So, What’s the Deal with Institutional Liquidity?

Onchain liquidity has always been a tough nut to crack, especially when compared to the long-standing TradFi models. But that’s where we come in. At Plume, we’re building bridges to bring more institutional capital on-chain, aligning the incentives, and making it easier for the big players to step into DeFi.

Our approach is straightforward: bring real, valuable assets like solar energy and stablecoins to the forefront, and make sure they’re accessible for both crypto-natives and institutional investors alike. It’s not just about following trends but creating a new wave of liquidity and utility that traditional markets can no longer ignore.

1) Tokenization is The Future

The Plume team is spearheading projects that solve real-world problems, like improving access to clean energy investments and boosting liquidity across borders. What sets Plume apart? We’re not just tokenizing for the sake of it but are building tools that institutions can actually use. From stablecoins to green finance, we’ve got our eye on products that drive adoption beyond the crypto space.

And Plume is not stopping there. Our partnerships, like those with Agora and other key players, are laying the groundwork for large-scale adoption. The goal is to digitize and tokenize every asset imaginable, making the RWA market as easy to access as any DeFi protocol.

2) Incentivizing Liquidity is The Real Challenge

The truth is, DeFi is where the yields are, and TradFi is looking over its shoulder. But what really needs to happen for this ecosystem to thrive is a flow of real capital into these markets. Plume’s efforts to incentivize on-chain liquidity aren’t just about making crypto look more attractive to TradFi players; it’s about showing them the superior flexibility and innovation that DeFi offers.

Chris Yin often says, “We’re not here to kill TradFi; we’re here to support it and evolve it.” The question is, how do we do that without falling into the trap of just rebranding old systems as “new”? By focusing on long-term value creation and practical, usable solutions, Plume is taking that extra step to bring the supply of institutional-grade assets to the DeFi market.

The RWAfi Ahead

Our North Star has always been clear: digitize, tokenize, and bring real assets on-chain. It’s a big goal, but Plume’s already making headway. The challenge is, how do we sustain and scale this growth? By staying true to the fundamentals, focusing on liquidity, and continuing to push the envelope on institutional engagement.

This is just the beginning. The Plume team is focused on building the future of RWA; expect to hear more about how we’re paving the way for tokenized assets to take center stage in both DeFi and TradFi.

Stay tuned — big moves are happening.

About Plume Network

Plume is the first fully integrated and modular chain focused on RWAfi. We’ve built the first modular and composable RWA-focused EVM-compatible chain designed to simplify onboarding for all types of assets and streamline capital onboarding through native infrastructure and RWAfi-specific features unified chain-wide. For the first time, we are enabling a composable ecosystem of defi built around RWAfis with an integrated end-to-end tokenization engine and a network of financial infrastructure partners for builders to plug and play. We have 165+ projects building on our private devnet today, with everything from private credit to equities, collectibles, and more.
